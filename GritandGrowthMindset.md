# Grit and Growth Mindset Assignment

---

## 1. Grit

### Question 1: Paraphrase the video in 1-2 lines

Grit refers to sticking to long-term goals and working towards them with passion and persistence, even when it gets tough. Success is more about working hard over time than having talent.

---

## 2. Introduction to Growth Mindset

### Question 2: Paraphrase the video in 1-2 lines

Having a growth mindset refers to the idea that ability and intelligence can be developed and improved. Being challenged and making mistakes can be opportunities for development and growth.

---

## 3. Understanding Internal Locus of Control

### Question 3:

#### What is Internal Locus of Control?

Internal Locus of Control refers to the idea that everything is up to you and your efforts, and nothing is up to outside forces or luck. 

#### What is the key point in the video?

The key point is to concentrate on and worry about only what you can control, which is your attitude and response, and not worry about other external factors.

## 4. How to Build a Growth Mindset

### Question 4: Key Points Mentioned by the Speaker

- Believe that skills can be developed  
- Embrace challenges  
- Learn from failures  
- Change “I can’t” to “I can’t yet”  
- Focus on effort, not just results  
- Take feedback positively  
- Practice regularly  
- Step out of your comfort zone  

---

## 5. My Action Plan to Build a Growth Mindset (as a Software Developer)

### Question 5: Ideas to Take Action

- Solve coding problems every day, even when tough  
- Review mistakes and understand why the solution failed  
- Ask for feedback on code and improve from it  
- Learn new technologies consistently instead of avoiding them  
- Break big goals into small, doable tasks  
- Check progress weekly  
- Avoid negative comparisons with others  
- Replace “This is too hard” with “This will make me better”  
- Build projects regularly, not just watch tutorials  
- Stay steady even when motivation is low
