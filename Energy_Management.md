# Energy Management Answers

## Question 1: What activities make you relax -- Calm quadrant?

-   Listening to soft music\
-   Taking a slow walk outside\
-   Sitting quietly without using my phone\
-   Talking calmly with a close friend\
-   Doing meditation or deep breathing\
-   Spending time in nature\
-   Reading a book for fun

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Question 2: When do you get into the Stress quadrant?

-   When I have many deadlines at the same time\
-   When I am learning something new and hard\
-   Before reviews or interviews\
-   When I delay my work and it becomes too much\
-   When I have problems or misunderstandings with someone

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Question 3: How do you know you are in the Excitement quadrant?

-   I feel motivated and full of energy\
-   I feel happy and interested in what I am doing\
-   Time goes fast because I enjoy the work\
-   I feel confident and positive\
-   I want to do more

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Question 4: Sleep is Your Superpower (Only Points)

-   Sleep improves memory and learning\
-   Sleep removes harmful waste from the brain\
-   Less sleep makes the immune system weak\
-   Sleep improves mood and mental health\
-   Poor sleep increases the risk of illness\
-   Sleep helps with emotional balance\
-   Deep sleep repairs the body\
-   Dream sleep helps creativity\
-   Adults need 7 to 9 hours of sleep\
-   Good sleep improves focus and work performance

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Question 5: Ideas to sleep better

-   Sleep and wake up at the same time every day\
-   Avoid using your phone before sleeping\
-   Do not drink coffee at night\
-   Keep the room dark and cool\
-   Do light exercise regularly\
-   Avoid heavy food before bed\
-   Practice meditation or deep breathing before sleep

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Question 6: Brain Changing Benefits of Exercise (Only Points)

-   Exercise improves memory and thinking\
-   Exercise helps the brain grow stronger\
-   Exercise reduces stress and worry\
-   Exercise improves mood by releasing happy chemicals\
-   Regular exercise protects the brain as we get older\
-   Exercise improves focus and attention\
-   Exercise helps you sleep better

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Question 7: Steps to exercise more

-   Start with small goals like 10 minutes a day\
-   Walk instead of using a vehicle for short distances\
-   Use stairs instead of the lift\
-   Join a sports activity\
-   Fix one time daily for exercise\
-   Exercise with friends to stay motivated\
-   Track your progress to stay regular
